Welcome to Binders website

A tablet application that tackles the challenges facing students while performing a research.


Binders takes advantage of many online resources to assist learners in finding & organizing content relevant to a particular topic. It can be also used by teachers to assemble a variety of content and share them with students to help them learn a topic.

Features & Roadmap

We are continually improving Binders to make it an even more valuable resource for learners and educators. We take your suggestions very seriously. Here is a  list of current features and roadmap of new ones.

Features
Perform automatic search on content relevant to a particular research topic. Take advantage of the most well-known online informational, educational and social media resources.

Enable users to enrich their binders and add content sources like a Facebook group page or a twitter hash tag.

Perform frequent search so that the content inside is continually updated. If there is a new YouTube video or a new twitter post, Binders automatically adds the new results to your binder and notifies you.

Pin important pieces of content. For example, the automatic search in YouTube resulted in 7 videos. Because the second video is very relevant to your research topic, you can pin this video so it remains fixed in your binder. Pinned content will be kept away from the live search pulled from the internet.

Add notes, configure the layout of your binder and move the content from one page to another.

 
Roadmap
Pin a piece of content directly from any internet page and adding it to your binder.

Add separators to divide your binders into chapters.

Record voice notes in addition to textual ones.

Perform a Backup of users' binders to the cloud.

Share your binder with your colleges.  Publish your binders to an online repository so that other users of Binders can access a copy of your binder and read your vision around the topic.
