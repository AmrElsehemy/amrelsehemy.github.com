Welcome to Binders website

A tablet application that tackles the challenges facing students while performing a research.


Binders takes advantage of many online resources to assist learners in finding & organizing content relevant to a particular topic. It can be also used by teachers to assemble a variety of content and share them with students to help them learn a topic.

Invitation to Beta Testers

We are seeking volunteers for beta testing of Binders. The objective of Binders beta testing is to provide an opportunity for end users to review and share feedback on the features and usability of the application.

If you are a teacher or a student, have an iPad and are interested to join our beta testing program, click on the invitation link below and fill the required information. We will contact you back with steps to download Binders on your iPad.


Your participation is highly important part of the development of Binders and is considered essential to the success of the product.
