Welcome to Binders website

A tablet application that tackles the challenges facing students while performing a research.


Binders takes advantage of many online resources to assist learners in finding & organizing content relevant to a particular topic. It can be also used by teachers to assemble a variety of content and share them with students to help them learn a topic.
